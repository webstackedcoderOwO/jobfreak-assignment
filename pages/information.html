Information

    The types of information are:
  • Factual information: data that can be verified or proven.
  • Conceptual information: data that explains ideas, theories, or principles.
  • Procedural information: data that instructs how to do something or follow a process.
  • Emotional information: data that expresses or influences feelings, moods, or attitudes.
  • Behavioral information: data that relates to what people do or how they act.
  • Attitudinal information: data that reflects opinions, beliefs, or values.
  • Motivational information: data that inspires, encourages, or persuades.
